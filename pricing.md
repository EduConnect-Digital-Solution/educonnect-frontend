# EduConnect Pricing Plans

Here is a detailed breakdown of the features available in our Basic and Premium plans. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive School Information System (SIS) that caters to the diverse needs of educational institutions.

---

## Basic Plan

**Price: ₦150,000 per term**

The Basic Plan is designed for schools that need a solid foundation for digitizing their core administrative and academic operations.

### Core Features:

*   **User Management**
    *   Admin dashboard for managing all user accounts (Admins, Teachers, Parents, Students).
    *   Secure user registration and authentication.
    *   Role-based access control.

*   **Student & Parent Portals**
    *   **Student Management:** Admins can manage student profiles, including demographic data and enrollment status.
    *   **Parent Dashboard:** Parents can view their children's basic profile and enrolled classes.
    *   **View Children:** Parents with multiple children in the school can access each child's profile from a single account.

*   **Academic Management**
    *   **Class Management:** Create and manage classes for different academic sessions.
    *   **Subject Management:** Assign subjects to classes and teachers.
    *   **Basic Gradebook:** Teachers can enter and manage student grades for assessments and exams.
    *   **Attendance Tracking:** Teachers can manually take and record student attendance for each class.

*   **Teacher Dashboard**
    *   **Class & Student Lists:** View assigned classes and the list of students in each class.
    *   **Profile Management:** Teachers can manage their own user profiles.

*   **School Administration**
    *   **School Profile Page:** Manage the school's official information.
    *   **Basic Analytics:** View simple analytics on User aaccounts and activity tracking with enrollment numbers and overall demographics.

*   **Support**
    *   **Onboarding & Training:** We will provide comprehensive training for your administrators and teachers to ensure a smooth transition.

---

## Premium Plan

**Price: ₦350,000 per term**

The Premium Plan is our all-inclusive package, offering advanced features for schools aiming for complete automation, enhanced communication, and deep analytical insights.

### Includes all features from the Basic Plan, plus:

*   **Advanced Academic Tools**
    *   **Automated Report Card Generation:** Automatically compile and generate professional, customizable report cards at the end of each term.
    *   **Advanced Gradebook:** Weighted grading, and detailed performance tracking across multiple assessment types.
    *   **Online Assignments & Submissions:** Teachers can create, distribute, and grade assignments through the platform. Students can submit their work online.
    *   **Exam & Timetable Management:** Schedule exams, and generate school-wide timetables.
    *   **Transcript Generation:** Automatically generate official student transcripts on demand.


*   **Enhanced Communication & Collaboration**
    *   **Internal Messaging System:** Secure messaging between Admins, Teachers, and Parents.
    *   **School-wide Announcements:** Broadcast announcements to all users or specific groups via the portal, email, and SMS.
    *   **Parent-Teacher Meeting Scheduler:** A built-in tool for parents to book meeting slots with teachers.
    *   **Digital Notice Board:** A central place for all school news, updates, and events.

*   **Advanced Analytics and Insights**
    *   **Student Performance Analytics:** In-depth analytics on student performance, identifying at-risk students and academic trends.
    *   **Teacher Performance Review:** Track and analyze teacher activities and student outcomes.
    *   **Enrollment & Admissions Analytics:** Track admission funnels, conversion rates, and forecast future enrollment.
    *   **Predictive Analytics:** AI-driven insights to predict student performance and identify areas for school improvement.

*   **Customization & Integration**
    *   **Custom Branding:** Use your school's logo, colors, and domain for a fully branded experience.
    *   **Data Import/Export:** Easily import data from spreadsheets or export your data for external use.

*   **Premium Support & Onboarding**
    *   **Dedicated Account Manager:** A single point of contact for all your needs.
    *   **Priority Support:** Get faster, priority responses to all your queries.
